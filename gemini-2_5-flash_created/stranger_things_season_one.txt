In a small town, a young boy goes missing. His mother, a woman who loves him, is very sad and tries to find him. The friends of the boy, a group of boys, also look for him. They find a strange girl with great power. This girl helps them search for the boy. There is a hidden place that is for the power of the town. This place opened a door to another dark world. Bad things come from this world. The bad things take the boy. The friends of the boy and the girl, along with his mother and the police leader of the town, work together. They fight the bad things and try to close the door. The girl uses her powers a lot. She saves her friends and helps to close the door. The boy is found and brought back home. But the girl is gone.
