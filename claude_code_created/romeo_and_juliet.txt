A Love Story That Ends In Death

This is a story about a boy and a girl who fall in love, even though their families have been fighting each other for as long as anyone can remember.

The boy is from one of the two great families in the city. The girl is from the other. The two families hate each other a great deal, though by the time the story begins no one is quite sure why.

The boy slips into a party at the home of the girl, where he sees her and falls completely in love. The two of them talk without being seen, in the dark outside her home, and quickly decide they want to be together for as long as they live. The next day, with the help of a kind older man who works for god, they get married without anyone knowing.

But things go wrong almost at once. The best friend of the boy gets into a fight with a young man from the family of the girl and is killed. The boy kills that young man in return, and is sent away from the city for doing it.

While the boy is gone, the parents of the girl try to force her to marry someone else. Not knowing what else to do, she goes back to the kind older man, who gives her a drink that makes her appear dead for a short time. The plan is for the boy to come back, find her in her resting place, and take her away when she wakes up. But the note telling the boy about the plan never reaches him.

The boy hears that the girl has died and rushes back to the city. Finding her body, he drinks something that kills him and dies beside her. The girl wakes up, finds the boy dead, and takes her own life.

Their families, seeing what their old hate has caused, finally end their fight. But it is too late for the two people they loved most.
