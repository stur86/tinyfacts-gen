In some lands, the people get to pick who leads them. They do this by all going out on the same day and saying who they want. The person who gets picked by the most people becomes the top leader of the land. This top leader is called the head of the land, and the day when people pick them is called picking day.

What makes this kind of leader different from other kinds is that the people are the ones who put them in the job, and they can only hold the job for a short, fixed time. In most lands it is four or five years. After that time, the people get to pick again. Sometimes they pick the same person a second time, but no more than that. This way, no one can hold on to the top job for ever.

This is very different from how things work in lands where the top leader is someone who was came into the world into the right family. In those places, the leader holds the job for as long as they live, and when they die, someone from their family takes over. The people in those lands do not get to have a say.

The head of the land has many jobs. They lead the people who fight for the land if a great fight starts. They work with a big group of people from all over the land to decide how to spend the money of the land, and what things all people must and must not do. They also speak for the whole land when meeting with the top leader of other lands.

Because the people gave this person the job, the person is supposed to work for the people. If they do not, the people can pick someone else next time. This makes the top leader answer to the people in a way that someone who got the job by being came into the world into the right family does not have to.
