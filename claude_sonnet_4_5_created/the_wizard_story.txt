A boy lives with his mean family in a small room under the stairs. He does not know that he is different. On the day he came into the world many years ago, a very big man comes and tells him that he can do things that other people can not do. He can make things happen just by wanting them to happen.

The boy goes to a school far away where other boys and girls also learn to do different things. The school is in a huge old building. The building has many hidden things and strange things. The boy makes two good friends at the school.

There is a bad man who wants to kill the boy. This bad man killed the mother and dad of the boy when the boy was very small. The boy has a mark on his head from when the bad man tried to kill him too. But the boy lived and the bad man lost his power.

Every year at school, something bad happens. The boy and his friends have to be strong and not afraid. They fight bad people and strange animals. They also learn more about the bad man and why he wants to kill the boy.

The boy learns that he and the bad man are joined in a strange way. To kill the bad man, the boy has to die too. The boy is strong and not afraid and lets the bad man kill him. But because of love, the boy does not really die. He comes back.

In the end, the boy and his friends fight the bad man and all the other bad people. Many people die in this big fight. But the good people beat them. The bad man dies and can never come back. The boy grows up and has his own children. He is finally safe and happy.
