The story begins with a boy who lives in a small town. He meets an old teacher who gives him a strange ball. The ball is bright and can give wishes when all seven are found. The boy sets out to find the other balls. He meets many friends: a strong girl, a strong fighter, a good child, and a quiet man. Together they go over land and water. They have many fights with a strong one, a strong one, and a dark group that wants the balls for power. The boy learns to use a bright power that lets him fly and throw fire. He trains hard, lifts heavy rocks, and runs fast. He learns to turn his power into a bright. In the end the boy and his friends took all the balls, stopped the dark group, and brought quiet to the world. The story shows that hard work, love, and a kind heart can get over fear.
