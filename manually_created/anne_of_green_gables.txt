A girl has no mother or dad. She has red hair. She goes to a far place to live with a man and a woman. The man and a woman wanted a boy but in the end they love her and she stays with them in their green house.

The girl loves to imagine things. She loves the beautiful place. Many things happen. She makes her hair green even though she did not want to. She makes her friend drunk. She also fights with a boy. The boy made fun of her for having red hair, so she hit him.

The girl wants to leave to go to a city and study. But one sad day, the man from the green house dies. The woman is alone and can not live easily. So the girl decides to not go to the city, and stay with her to help her.
