To black something means to make it the color of the night. You make it very dark so no one can see what is under it.

People often do this to words on paper. If there are things that should be hidden, they use something to write over the words. Then, when you look at the paper, you see only long black lines. The words are gone from your eyes and no one can read them.

You can also black your shoes. People do this to make their old shoes look new and bright again. 

To black a person is something else. It usually means you do not let them join a group or get a job. If many groups of business agree to black a worker, that worker can not find work in any place. This is a very hard situation for that person and makes life very hard for them.

Sometimes, the brain of a person can black out. This happens when they suddenly fall or can not see. For a short time, everything is dark to them. When they wake up, they do not remember what happened. It is like a piece of time is missing.

You can also make the name of a person black. This does not use real color. It means you say bad things about them to other people. You try to make their name look bad instead of good. You want people to think the person is not nice. This can hurt them for a long time.
