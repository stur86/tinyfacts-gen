To block something means to stop it from moving or going forward. You put something in the way so the first thing can not pass.

Think about a door. If you put a heavy chair in front of a door, you block the door. No one can come inside. If you are playing a game with a ball, you might use your hands or feet to block the ball. You stop it from hitting the place it wants to go.

You can also block people on a computer or a phone. When you block someone, they can not talk to you. They might try to send you words, but those words will never arrive. You will not see their face or hear their voice. Many people do this when someone is being mean or annoying. It helps you stay calm and happy.

Sometimes your brain can block things too. If something very bad happens, your brain might block the memory. This means you can't remember the bad thing even if you try. It is a way your body tries to keep you safe from feeling sad.

Also, you might feel blocked when you are trying to work. You sit at your desk and want to write or draw, but your mind feels empty. No ideas come to you. You feel stuck. This happens to many people who make books or beautiful things.
