A book is a thing you can hold in your hands. It is made of many pieces of paper. These pieces are held together at one side. On the paper, there are many words. Sometimes there are pictures, too.

People use books to tell stories. Some stories are about things that really happened in the world. Other stories are made up in the brain of another person. You can read about a girl with red hair or a boy who flies.

Some books are for learning. They tell us about how the world works. They talk about animals, trees, and the stars. Teachers use these books in schools to help children know more.

Long ago, books were very hard to make. A person had to write every word by hand. This could take many months. Today, we use big things we built to make many books very quickly. Now, almost everyone can have them.

Some books are not made of paper anymore. You can read them on a computer or a phone. We still call these things books.

When you read a book, you can see far places while sitting in your chair. You can know what people who lived a hundred years ago thought. It is like a conversation with someone you have never met.
