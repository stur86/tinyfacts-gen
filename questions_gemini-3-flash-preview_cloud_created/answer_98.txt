Sometimes people use the word "body" in a way that is not about parts of you like your arms and legs. To body someone means to beat them in a way that is very complete. You do so well that the other person looks like they can't even move or fight back.

Imagine you are playing a game with your friends. If you beat them and the other person loses in a way that is not even close, you body them. You are much better than they are. You take control and do not let them have a chance. You are huge while they are very small.

You can also body a job or something you have to do. If you do it in a way that is perfect and you are quick, you body it. It means you are the best worker at that thing.

If two people are writing songs and trying to see who is better, and one person writes something that is much better, people might say they body the other person. Their words are more beautiful and their voice is stronger.

People say this to show they have power. When you body someone, you are showing that you are much stronger or better than they are at that moment. It makes you the one who is on top, and it makes the other person look like they are not even there. It is a way to say you beat them completely.
