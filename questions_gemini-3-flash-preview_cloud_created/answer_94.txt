Red Water Inside You

Blood is the red water inside humans and animals. It moves all around you through small lines. Your heart is in your chest and it works to keep the blood moving. Blood is very important because it carries good things like food and air to every part of your body. It also takes away things that you do not need anymore.

Blood is made of many parts. Most of it is a clear water that carries everything along. Inside this water are many tiny cells. There are more red cells than any other kind. These red cells carry the air you breathe to your head, your arms, and your legs. Without these red cells, you could not live.

Other parts of blood are white. These are like a group of people who fight. They look for bad things that might make you sick and they attack them. They keep you safe and help you stay well.

There are also very tiny bits in your blood that help if you get a cut. When you are hurt and blood comes out, these tiny bits stick together to block the opening. They stop the blood from leaving your body and make a hard cover so your skin can grow back.

Not everyone has the same kind of blood. There are different types. If someone is very hurt and needs more blood, a doctor must find the right type for them. If the types are not a good match, it can cause big problems.
