A blink is when you shut your eyes and open them again very fast. This happens many times every day. Most of the time, you do not even notice that you are doing it.

Why do we blink? One reason is to keep our eyes wet. There is a little bit of water in our eyes. When we blink, the skin over our eyes moves this water around. This keeps the eyes clean and keeps them from getting too dry.

Another reason is to guard the eyes. If something small flies toward your face, your brain tells your eyes to shut fast. This stops the small thing from hitting your eye.

People blink many times every minute. But when you look at a computer, you might blink less. This can make your eyes feel tired or dry. It is weird, but babies do not blink as much as older people do.

Sometimes we blink because we are nervous or because the light is too bright. It is a small thing, but it helps us see the world.
