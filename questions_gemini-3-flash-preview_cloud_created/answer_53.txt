An aunt is a woman in your family. She is the sister of your mother or the sister of your father. Also, the wife of your uncle is your aunt.

An aunt is often a person who loves you. She is not your mother, but she cares for you. Some aunts live near your home. They might visit for dinner or help your parents. Other aunts live far away in another city. You might see them during the summer or at a big family party.

An aunt might have children. You can play with those children when you see her. They are also part of the family.

Aunts can tell you interesting things. They knew your father or mother when they were very young. An aunt can tell you a story about when your dad was a small boy. She might tell you what your mom liked to do or how she acted when she was a child.

If your aunt is the sister of the mother of your mother, she is a great aunt. She may be very old.

People like having aunts because they are kind. An aunt is an important person in your life.
