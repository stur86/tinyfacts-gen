A blonde is a person with very light hair. This hair color is not dark like black or brown. It looks almost like the light from the sun.

A long time ago, many blondes lived in cold places with not much sun. Their bodies had less of the thing that makes color. This helped them live better in those cold areas.

Many children have blonde hair when they are very young. But as they grow, their hair often gets darker. By the time they are men or women, their hair might be brown.

Some people want blonde hair even if they do not have it. They use something to take the dark color out of their hair. They think it makes them look beautiful.

People around the world have many different hair colors. Some have hair as black as night, and some have hair as light as the day. Being blonde is just one way a person can look.
