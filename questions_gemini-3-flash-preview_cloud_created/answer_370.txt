Five is how many things you have when you have one more than four. It is the number after four and before six.

If you look at one hand, you see five fingers. This is why the number five is easy to understand. We see it on our bodies every day. If you have two hands, you have two groups of five. This makes ten.

A star has five points that reach out. You can draw a star using five lines. Sometimes, a small group of friends or a team has five people. They can play games together. When you and a friend are happy, you might hit your hands together and say high five.

When you are five years old, you are not a baby anymore. You are becoming a big child. You might start to go to school to learn. If you have five pieces of sweet food and give them to your friends, everyone will be happy. Five is a good number because it is in the middle when you are numbering from one up to ten.
