Most things like cups or boxes have a bottom. It is the part that sits on the ground. To bottom something can mean to reach the very end of it, like when you drink all the water in a bottle. You have reached the bottom.

But people also use this word to talk about how they act with each other. When two people are very close and doing things with their bodies, one person may be the top and the other may be the bottom. To bottom means to be the person who takes what the other person gives. The person at the top is usually the one who leads what is happening.

To bottom is a way that someone decides to act. It does not mean a person is not strong. In fact, it can take a lot of heart to be a bottom. It is about trusting another person. The bottom allows the top to have the power for some time. This makes both people feel good.

Some people always like to bottom, while others like to change. Knowing what you like helps you find a good match. It is a way for people to talk about what they want when they are together.
