A fell is a name for a high area of land. You can find this kind of land in the top part of the world where it is often cold. It is like a large rock that reaches toward the sky.

A long time ago, people came across the water and brought this word with them. In the way they spoke, the word meant a high place or a rock.

On a fell, the air is often cold. The wind can be very strong, and it rains a lot. Even during the summer, it is a good idea to wear a jacket if you go to the top. The ground can be very wet and hard to walk on.

You will see many animals on a fell. There are white animals with thick hair that people use to make clothes. These animals walk around and eat the green from the ground all day.

People also go to these places to walk. They follow the tracks to the very top. From the top, they can see a long way. They see bodies of water and many other high places. It is a very quiet and beautiful place to be.
