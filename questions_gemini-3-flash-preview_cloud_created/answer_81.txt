Being Big

A boy is not tall. He wants to be big. He finds a box at a park. The box can make wishes come true. The boy tells the box he wants to be big. 

The next morning, the boy wakes up. He is not a boy anymore. He is a man! His mom is afraid because she does not know him. He has to run away to the city and find a job. 

He works at a place that makes fun things for children. He is very good at his job because he still thinks like a child. He becomes a leader and makes a lot of money. He even has a girlfriend. But in the end, he misses his mom and his home. He finds the box again and wishes to be a boy one more time. He goes home and he is happy to be small again.
