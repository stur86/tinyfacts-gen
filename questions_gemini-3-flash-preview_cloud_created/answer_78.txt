The word besides is used to show more than one thing or reason.

One way to use it is to mean as well as. Imagine you have a dog and a cat. You could say, besides my dog, I have a cat. This means you have both animals.

Another way to use it is when you explain why you do something. If you do not want to go out, you might have many reasons. You might feel tired. You might also see that it is raining. You can say, I am too tired to go out. Besides, it is raining. Here, the word helps you add another reason for what you decided to do.

Many people use this word when they are talking with their friends. It is a good word to use when you want to add more to what you are already saying.
