A block is a hard thing with straight sides. Most blocks have six sides that are all the same. They are often made of wood or heavy rock.

Children love to play with small blocks. They put one on top of another to build tall things or tiny houses. Because the sides are straight, the blocks stay where you put them. If you push the tall things you built, they will fall and make a loud noise.

Blocks come in many colors. Some are red, some are blue, and some are green. This makes them look very nice when they are all together.

There are also very big blocks. People use these big blocks to make walls for a large building. These blocks are much stronger and heavier than the ones children play with. It takes many people or big things that work to move them from place to place.

To block also means to stop something from moving. If you stand in a door, you block the way. No one can get past you. A big fallen tree can block a road so cars can not go.
